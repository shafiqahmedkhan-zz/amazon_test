3.0 out of 5 starsFour hour burn time - but they often go out
By CatsAndBooks TOP 100 REVIEWER on 14 Nov. 2016
Colour: WhiteItem Package Quantity: 1 Verified Purchase
They burn steadily and work well in an essential oil evaporator. Unlike some other brands, they don't fill the air with a chemical smell.

The burning time is just a little over 4 hours, which is good. However, they don't burn reliably. Very often, they simply go out. The slightest waft of air, or movement of the candle holder, and pfft, the flame is out. I've compared this with other tea lights of different brands, and these are indeed unreliable.

The metal cups are all badly dented. This isn't a problem for use in an essential oil evaporator or in a glass bowl candle holder, but it would be if they're intended for decoration.

5.0 out of 5 starsLight up with traditional tea lights
By smartyfun TOP 500 REVIEWER on 28 Jan. 2017
Colour: WhiteItem Package Quantity: 1 Verified Purchase
I must have used over one thousand of these IKEA tea light candles over the years, and never had a dud! They normally light first time and produce a small, bright and consistent flame.
They are definately unscented and don't produce any smoke under normal conditions.
As they don't contain any animal ingredients they shouldn't be a problem for vegetarians, vegans and others with a similar sensitivity.
You need to use them sensibly and use your common sense to prevent accidents. Also, the aluminium case can get hot so watch where you place them to prevent scorching.
